Plato is waiting for you.

How to play Mastermind:

This is a code-breaking game.
Match the colours of the code set by Plato (hidden from you) to win.
No blank options are allowed.

When you're done with your selection, click on the EVAL button.
The pegs on the left indicate the correctness of your choice.

  • The pegs go in a Z sequence.
  • Black means both the position and colour of your choice are correct.
  • Red means the colour, but not the position, is correct.
  • If no pegs are placed, it means you've gotten it very wrong.

You have five tries.
If you use up all your tries before you can guess Plato's code correctly, you lose.

All the best!
